Cooking:

From 8 to 22 grams of freshly ground coffee
is poured into the coffee maker holder, carefully
leveled and then pressed with a tamper so that
the ground coffee is formed into an even tablet.
Then, water heated to a temperature of 88–97
°C is passed through the resulting tablet under
a pressure of 9 bar. with a volume of about 30
milliliters, it was extracted in 21-30 seconds
(after 1.5-2.5 seconds of pre-infusion) and had
a mass of 31-40 grams (the mass of classic esp
resso). The ratio of suitable grinding, suitable
holder by creating a force of 150-200 newtons),
suitable water temperature and its stability, as
(about 8-12 or 18-22 grams, single and double
holder respectively) is the key to a good result.
